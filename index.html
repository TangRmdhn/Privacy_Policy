Privacy Policy for SyncJadwal

Last Updated: October 17, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to SyncJadwal ("the Application"). This web application is designed to help students of UPN "Veteran" Yogyakarta synchronize their class schedules from the BIMA academic portal directly to their personal Google Calendar.

This Privacy Policy explains how we access, use, and handle your data when you use our service.

2. Information We Access

When you grant access, our Application interacts with your Google Account to use the following Google API Scope:

  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/calendar: This permission allows the Application to create new calendar events (your class schedules) on your primary Google Calendar.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your Google user data is used exclusively for the single, user-facing feature of the Application:

  • We access your Google Calendar **only** to create calendar events based on the schedule data you paste into the Application.
  • We **do not** read, store, or analyze any of your existing calendar events.
  • We **do not** share, sell, or transfer your data to any third parties for any purpose, such as advertising or marketing.

4. Data Storage

SyncJadwal **does not store your personal data or Google Calendar data**. The authentication token (session ID) is held temporarily in your browser during your session and is not stored permanently on our servers. The entire process is transactional; we receive your schedule, send it to the Google Calendar API on your behalf, and then discard the data.

5. Compliance with Google Policies

SyncJadwal's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
